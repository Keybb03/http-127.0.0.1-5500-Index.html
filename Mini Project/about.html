About West Coast Grooming


West Coast Grooming opened in 2022 with the goal of providing top level dog grooming services for the community of Nanaimo. We love dogs and pride ourselves in pampering your pet and making them feel loved. Regular grooming keeps your pet's coat smelling fresh and looking at its best!

Our grooming and bathing service includes ear cleaning, nail cutting, and teeth cleaning, as well as your choice of several professional shampoos. Our goal is to meet your every need with our wide array of services. Enhance your pet's health and beauty by calling to schedule an appointment today. Learn about our grooming services here.
